About Us

DSC Manipal strives to be the single focal point of overall development and growth of student community with interests in areas of data analytics, machine learning, and big data.

Services

What We Do.

We provide a platform for students to collaborate and explore new technologies, engage with industry professionals through seminars, conduct hands-on workshops on tools and techniques used in various fields of machine learning.

Hands-on Workshops

We conduct project-based workshops on different disciplines of machine learning. So, at the end of the workshop, you'll have a portfolio project to show.

Guest Seminars

We'll bring in engineers and eminent personalities working in the field of the data science and machine learning to talk about their work. This will also provide networking opportunities for the students.

Internal Projects

The DSC Manipal team will work on deployable, end-to-end applications in different fields of machine learning, including but not limited to NLP and Computer Vision.

Certifications

We'll bring in companies whose certifications matter. These companies will provide training and offer certificates upon successfull completion of the course.
